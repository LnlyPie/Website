Why us?

Free As In Freedom

We aim to create a complete and free games. Even better. All the content in our games (textures, etc.) can be used by you in any way you want. Just give us some credit ;).

Open Source

All of our games are Open Source. That means you can change in them anything you want. Well... Anything that's legal. Just follow the License restrictions and you'll be fine. Most of our games use the MIT License.

Building a Community

We try to build a Coummunity. If you want to help, just join our Discord and be yourself. We accept everyone here. (just follow the rules please).
